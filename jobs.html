💡 Quick Tips for Job Seekers

  • Top Portals: Naukri.com, LinkedIn, Indeed, Internshala (freshers), sarkariresult.com
  • Resume: Tailor for ATS — use keywords matching the job description.
  • Govt Deadlines: Vacancies fill fast and portals can crash near deadline. Apply 2–3 days early.
  • UP-Specific: Watch UPPSC, UP Police, UP Metro, and UP Basic Education Board notifications.
  • Free Prep: YouTube channels like Unacademy, StudyIQ, and Adda247 cover govt exam syllabus for free.
